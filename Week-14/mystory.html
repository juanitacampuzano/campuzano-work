My Story:

I was born in Medellin, Colombia. The first time we came to Miami I was 4 years old and moved back and forth till I was 7 years old. I grew up in Miami, Florida with my father, mother, sister, and nanny (who later became like my second mom). I graduated highschool at Palmer Trinity with High Honors and moved to New York City to pursue my passion in the creative field. I am currently now a Junior at Parsons, The New School for Design. I major in Strategic Design and Management while minoring in Communication Design. I am expected to graduate spring 2019.
